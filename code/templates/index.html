About Data

This data has been obtained from the Japanese colonial museum in Korea. By collaborating with the museum, we were successful in initiating a survey that would ask visitors of the museum about their overview and thoughts about the museum.

The survey is categorized into four questions, where the survey would ask the visitors about their satisfaction scores, nationality, age range and personal opinions. Furthermore in order to express their personal opinions through graphs, personal opinions were evaluated by utilizing the sentiment scores.
